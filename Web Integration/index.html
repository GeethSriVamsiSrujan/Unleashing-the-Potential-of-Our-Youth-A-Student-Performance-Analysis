Frequently Asked Questions

What is student performance analysis?

Student performance analysis helps in understanding the influence of important factors such as parental level of education, the status of test preparation course etc. on the performance of the students in the exams.

Why do we need student performance analysis?

This helps in identifying dropouts and students who require special attention, enabling teachers to provide appropriate advising and counseling

How to analyse a student performance?

Student's performance can be analysed with the help of various tools like Creating assignments, Creating exams, Using classroom assessment techniques,Using concept maps,Using concept tests.

How can this analysis helps students?

This Analysis help students to think critically, creatively, and logically. They enable students to evaluate information from different sources, identify patterns and trends, and draw conclusions and recommendations.

In what ways student analysis influence the student education and development?

Data analysis helps teachers understand their students' learning abilities and challenges, and facilitates an ingrained cultural process that uses detailed inputs (information) to ensure optimal outputs
